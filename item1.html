7 Minutes

7 Minutes is a demo game made during Industry Project at university. Done in a team of 4, I was the Lead Programmer and Project Manager. The game itself was developed using Unity3D and all assets and sound were made by members of the team. Being the main programmer I created a majority of the player character, including the animations and mechanics. I also developed the entire orb character, which acts as a light mechanic for the platformer. Being the project manager I had to organise the team and creeate weekly sprints among other duties.
