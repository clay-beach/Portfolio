7 Minutes

Project Manager | Lead Programmer

7 Minutes is a demo game made during Industry Project at university. Done in a team of 4, I was the Lead Programmer and Project Manager. The game itself was developed using Unity3D and all assets and sound were made by members of the team. Being the main programmer I created a majority of the player character, including the animations and mechanics. I also developed the entire orb character, which acts as a light mechanic for the platformer. Being the project manager I had to organise the team and create weekly sprints among other duties such as time management.

DMS Travel

Developer

First Person Shooter

Lead Programmer

A game made using Unity3D for a university assignment. I was the lead programmer, developing the player character, scoring system, UI and AI. The AI are programmed using a navmesh and pathfinding algorithim with varying attack range, speed and durability, depending on the AI type. The player is created with the Unity character controller and has a set health, speed, damage and ammo count. The player stats and ammo can be enhanced with buffs located on the map for a short period of time. The game has 3 levels of increasing difficulty with new AI spawns each round.
